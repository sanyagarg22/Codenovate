Codenovate
Inspiring kids to innovate with code.

Learn more about us

Codenovate is a student-run nonprofit organization whose goal is to teach mobile app development, python, and AI to underrepresented kids and inspire them to pursue STEM and computer science in the future.

We believe that by breaking down complex concepts into simple building blocks accompanied with fun activities and projects, we can encourage kids to start exploring technology. We have found that mobile apps are the most effective and efficient way of introducing computer science to younger kids, as they provide a tangible interface that they can relate to. As most kids have previous experience playing with mobile apps, integrating them into CS curriculum keeps them interested and motivated, as they see them in use everyday.

Since the launch of our nonprofit organization more than a year ago, we have successfully completed 3 mobile app development programs at a local San Jose library and we plan to extend it to include AI as well. Different branches of Codenovate are currently running in over 3 libraries across California.
