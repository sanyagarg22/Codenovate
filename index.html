We are a student-run 501(c)3 nonprofit organization with the goal to make computer science all-inclusive. We teach mobile app development, python, and AI to underrepresented kids in technology in effort to inspire them to pursue STEM and computer science in the future.

We believe that by breaking down complex concepts into simple building blocks accompanied with fun activities and projects, we can encourage kids to start exploring technology and genuinely enjoy it. We have found that mobile apps are the most effective and efficient way of introducing computer science to younger kids, as they provide a tangible interface that they can relate to. As most kids have previous experience playing with mobile apps, integrating them into CS curriculum keeps them interested and motivated, as they see them in use everyday.
