TechGalz

TechGalz is a comprehensive series in which our goal is to cultivate an interest in technology and entrepreneurship in young girls.

We teach girls AppInventor basics along with the foundations of entrepreneurship to prepare them to participate in Technovation, the largest female technology-entrepreneurship competition. We first teach these principles, and continue on to mentoring and supporting girls throughout the Technovation season.

Technovation offers girls around the world the opportunity to learn the skills they need to emerge as tech entrepreneurs and leaders. Girls work in teams to build both a mobile app and a business plan to launch that app, supported by our volunteers and guided by our curriculum.

Appnovators

Appnovators is an 8-week series that will fast track its students into developing cool android mobile applications. The primary programming language Appnovators will cover is AppInventor, a basic drag-and-drop app development platform. No programming experience is needed to participate in Appnovators.

AInnovators

AInnovators is a comprehensive 10-week series that introduces middle schoolers to Python and basic AI algorithms. We will explore the many dimensions of AI including applications in the medical, aerospace, and robotics fields and discuss the ethics and morals behind artificial intelligence.

We start with the basics of python and move on to focus on the algorithms and steps to create Artificial Intelligence programs.
