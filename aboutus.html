About Us

Codenovate is a student-run nonprofit organization whose our goal is to cultivate an interest in coding technology in young kids. We strive to present our concepts with fun and engaging activities to inspire kids to work with tech in the future.

We do this with three programs across various libraries: TechGalz, Appnovators, and AInnovators.

  • TechGalz encourages girls to participate in STEM by teaching them mobile app develpment and entrepreunurship in preparation to participate in Technovation.
  • Appnovators is aimed to teach young kids AppInventor, a simple drag-and-drop mobile app programming language.
  • AInnovators exposes kids to Python and the foundations of AI algorithms and applications.
