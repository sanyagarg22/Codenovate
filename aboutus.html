About Us

Codenovate is a student-run 501(c)3 nonprofit organization whose goal is to cultivate an interest in coding technology in young kids. We strive to present our concepts with fun and engaging activities to inspire kids to work with tech in the future.

We do this various programs across multiple libraries. These are some of our most popular: Appnovators, AInnovators, and Diving Deeper Into Java.

  • Diving Deeper Into Java expands on preliminary Java concepts to further students into the pipeline of computer science by exposing them to advanced computer science topics.
  • Appnovators is aimed to teach young kids AppInventor, a simple drag-and-drop mobile app programming language.
  • AInnovators exposes kids to Python and the foundations of AI algorithms and applications.
